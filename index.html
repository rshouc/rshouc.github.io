Basic Information

I am a lecturer at Prof. Bo HE's UVL(Underwater Vehicle Lab) Lab in Ocean University of China. Before that, I received my PhD degree from Xidian University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Huixin Zhou. My research interests include computer vision, infrared image processing, AUV system design, etc.
